## Overview

Topaz Video AI Professional Suite is a complete video enhancement platform with batch processing, preview comparison, and custom model selection for professional video workflows.

Batch processing queues multiple videos for overnight enhancement, handling the long processing times that AI video enhancement requires.

Preview comparison shows side-by-side before/after comparison for any frame, enabling quality evaluation before committing to full video processing.

Custom model selection provides multiple AI models optimized for different content types — animation, faces, landscapes, and archival footage — for optimal results per source material.

Topaz Video AI Professional Suite is the video enhancement platform for professionals who need AI video processing with the batch capability, preview, and model selection that production video workflows demand.

## Performance & Integration

Topaz Video AI is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Topaz Video AI processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
